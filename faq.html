Profile Check In Calendar Charts FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Question: Where should I start?

Answer: Start by setting up your profile and completing your first check-in.

Question: What should I do daily?

Answer: Your daily check-in

Question: Will I recieve a notification?

Answer: Yes, if you selected to recieve notifications.

Question: What should I enter in the journal?

Answer: Your daily thoughts, feelings, diet routine, exercise routine, daily medications, daily vitals.

Question: How can I see previous journal entries?

Answer: Through your calender , you can access previous journal entries

Question: How can I see my emotions for a period of time?

Answer: Your Charts track your emotions for the last 30 days

Question: How can I see my water intake for a period of time?

Answer: Your Charts track your water intake for the last 30 days
